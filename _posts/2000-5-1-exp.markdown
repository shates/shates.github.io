* __2016 – 2017__
*Field Crew Intern*

Northwest Entomological Research Center in Corvallis, Oregon

Project contribution: 1) Indirect effects of herbicidal spray levels on insect and
arthropod biodiversity in clear-cut logging plots. 2) Aquatic arthropod sampling of a future
community lake restoration site.
Gained skills in family-level insect identification, species-level plant identification, using GPS units on the ground, and general field work practices.

*Volunteer*

Natural History Museum of Los Angeles County

Project contribution to the Citizen Science BioScan Project: identified scuttle fly (family: Phoridae) biodiversity in Los Angeles County using dichotomous keys for species-level identification in the genus *Megaselia*
Interacted with museum guests in the main museum as an "On the floor" volunteer; science and history communication based on museum trainings.
