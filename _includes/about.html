About Tessa


I am atmospheric scientist interested in clouds and precipitation in the mid and high latitudes, radar, and surface meteorological measurements. I am especially interested in using ground-based observations to improve our understanding of precipitation processes in glaciated and mixed phase clouds, and how we can use ground-based findings to improve satellite retrievasls of precipitation.

I am a PhD student in Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences at UW-Madison. Previously, I earned my BS in Earth System Science and BA in Spanish at UC Irvine.
